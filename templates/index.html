Attrition Risk

Using ML models to predict attrition

Our Methodology

This application helps to predict whether the employee is at the risk of leaving or not. It demonstrates the system's and ML model's ability to anaylyse data points and forecast employee turnover.

Data Ingestion

The data is either entered or a csv file is uploaded. In csv format it is desgined to handle missing values and scale data effectively.

Model Training

We leverage a proprietary of various models like AdaBoost, DT, KNN, LR, GB, RF, XGB, SVM trained specifically on historical attrition factors, ensuring high predictive accuracy for employee risk scoring.

Explainability

To provide explainability graphs are plotted and the feature affecting the attrition is shown.
